The Great Favaro Hnos family

Eximcruz is a family-owned company, founded on February 22, 2011 by Edwin Carlo, David Carlo, and Cruz Carlo. Previously, we had a successful experience in selling coffee and decided to expand by creating a company that specializes in the processing and marketing of the Amazon nut in the international market.
